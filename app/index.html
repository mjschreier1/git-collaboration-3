Making a Manhattan

  1. Add 3.75 oz of bourbon or rye to a shaker
  2. Add 1.5 oz of sweet vermouth
  3. Add 2 dashes of Angostura bitters
  4. Add a teaspoon of cherry juice
  5. Add ice to the shaker and shake aggressively for about 20 seconds
  6. Strain into a martini glass
  7. Garnish with 2 Luxardo cherries
